Here we answer some of your most asked questions.

  • What is Jimmy clan?

    Jimmy clan is just a group of people who want to hang out and talk. Founded by William and Luke in 2022.

  • How do i join?

    If you want to join us, Click the Join button on the top right corner of the page. Fill out the form, and wait for it to be approved. Once approved, a confirmation email will be sent to you.

  • Whats are the Perks of Jimmy Clan?

    Perks can include but not limited to: Free food or Discounted food, Playtime on Other Jimmy clans electronic devices and last but not least Fun events hosted almost daily!
